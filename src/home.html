NyayNari is dedicated to women’s rights and women’s laws. We aim to raise awareness about the lack of women's rights in India and advocate for gender equality. In India, women face numerous challenges and discrimination in various aspects of life, including education, employment, healthcare, and personal safety.


Despite legal protections and initiatives, gender-based violence, including domestic violence, sexual harassment, and dowry-related crimes, continues to be prevalent. Women also face barriers in accessing education and employment opportunities, limiting their economic empowerment and social mobility.
